what is empi?

EMPI is a mobile app that has some cool features.

AI-Powered Chatbot

The knowledge base of the chatbot contains answers to popular questions about inclusion, parenting and communication. Learn more.

Catalogue of Social Stories

The Social Stories are comic stripes or short novels about behavior and communication. Learn more.

Favorites and Notes

Users can save their favorites Social Stories and make notes. This tool can be used together with a psychiatrist. Learn more.

Planning Calendar

Proper day planning is an important part of inclusive education. The calendar will anounce users of upcoming local charity events. Learn more.
